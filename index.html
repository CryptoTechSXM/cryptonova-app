How to Register
  • Connect MetaMask Click "Connect Wallet" in the top-right. Make sure you're on Base Sepolia testnet.
  • Get Test USDC On testnet, the MockUSDC contract minted $10,000 to the deployer. Ask for a test transfer, or mint directly.
  • Approve USDC Allow the Matrix contract to spend $10 USDC on your behalf. One MetaMask confirmation.
  • Register Confirm the register transaction. You'll be placed into the BFS matrix automatically.
  • Earn & Withdraw As new members register, USDC flows through the chain to you. Withdraw any time from the Dashboard.
