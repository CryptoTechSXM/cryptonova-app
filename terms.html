2. Nature of the Protocol

CryptoNova is a decentralized, autonomous smart contract system deployed on the Base blockchain (and BNB Chain). It operates without a central administrator, custodian, or intermediary. Once deployed, the core logic is immutable and cannot be altered by any party.

The protocol implements a binary matrix compensation structure where participants pay an entry fee in USDC/USDT and receive payments from subsequent participants according to predefined on-chain rules. The CNOVA token is distributed as an in-protocol reward and is not a security or investment contract.
